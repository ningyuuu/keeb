What makes a quality mechanical keyboard better?
Much effort and love is put into every keyboard I make. Here’s a list of things I do, as compared to an off-the-shelf keyboard.
  1. Quality switches. Lubed switches make keypresses smooth like butter.
  2. Better stabilisers. Tuned stabilisers make a satisfiying spacebar click.
  3. Quality keycaps, for better and key feel and that sexy thock.
  4. Good cases make the keyboard feel premium and kickass.
Plus, tons of peripherals, from wires to novelty keycaps, that let you express your individuality!
