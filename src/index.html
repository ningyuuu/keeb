Why Ningzy Works?
As a software engineer, I spend most of my awake hours in front of a computer typing at a keyboard.

I found that retail keyboards are overpriced, especially for their subpar quality, while the good ones were beyond the budget I was willing to pay for.

This is why I did my homework and learned to build a quality keyboard for myself. The result was a keyboard I am proud of, and enjoyed typing on as a daily driver.

Now, I’d like to share my passion for keyboards with everyone, and tailor the perfect keyboard for you!
